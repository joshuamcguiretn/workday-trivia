The Year Edition includes:

- 52 paid trivia rounds, separate from the free starter pack
- 52 presenter decks in native Google Slides format

- Tie-breakers for friendly competition
- Quick facts and Team Spark prompts to encourage light conversation

- Designed for roughly 5 minutes per round

## License summary
The $299 Year Edition license covers one manager-led team or recurring group. The buyer may use the materials internally with that team, present them live, print host materials, and copy files for internal team use. The buyer may not resell, redistribute, publicly post, or repackage the files as their own product.
